### Step 2: Supplement Addition (Aseptic)

1. Add the **Insulin, Transferrin, and Selenium** (ITS) components.
2. Add **FGF2-G3**. *Note: Due to G3 stability, this media will remain potent for 7 days at 37¬∞C.*
